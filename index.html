The Process

Three Steps.
Zero Risk.

01
Sign NCND Agreement
Both parties independently sign a standard Non-Circumvention Non-Disclosure agreement. All identities remain protected. Zero commitment or cost required at this stage from either side.
02
Receive Introduction
Upon NCND execution by both parties, we facilitate a direct confidential introduction. We remain present in all communications and coordinate the due diligence process throughout.
03
Agreement Signed
Both parties negotiate terms directly and execute a binding offtake agreement. Our 2% facilitator fee is charged to the producer at this point only. Zero cost to the buyer at any stage.
