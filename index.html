India's current NdPr import dependence on China — diversification is urgent and strategic

India's Ministry of Heavy Industries has issued an RFP for integrated sintered NdFeB magnet manufacturing — the backbone of electric vehicles, wind turbines, and defence systems.

Five manufacturers will be selected. Only three receive guaranteed NdPr oxide from state-owned IREL. Winners 4 and 5 face zero domestic supply — and a 7-year production obligation that legally requires them to source independently.

Brazil's rare earth producers offer the ideal non-Chinese supply solution, officially endorsed by the February 2026 Modi-Lula critical minerals MoU.

The Process

How We Facilitate
Your Supply Agreement
